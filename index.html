About Your Councillor

Healthcare Expertise Meets Public Service

Elected in May 2023, I serve the residents of Wednesbury South with a focus on combining my professional healthcare expertise with a passion for public service.

"My role on the Health and Adult Social Care Scrutiny Board allows me to ensure our local healthcare services are efficient and accessible for all Sandwell residents."

With a Master of Science in Medicine from Cardiff University, I have spent years working at the intersection of health and community. Before joining the Council, I served as the International Members Representative for the Faculty of Homeopathy for nearly six years, where I advocated for global healthcare standards.


Currently, I also serve as a Director at Maxwell Pain Clinic Ltd in Birmingham, focusing on improving the quality of life for those suffering from chronic pain.

Dedicated Space: Wednesbury South

Focusing on What Matters Most

In partnership with local residents and data from the Sandwell Residents Survey, my current focus areas for our ward are:

Cleaner Neighborhoods

Implementing strict measures to reduce fly-tipping and keep our streets clean. A cleaner environment improves community pride and public health.

Safer Transit

Working to improve highways and road safety for both motorists and pedestrians. Ensuring safe routes to schools and transport links is a priority.

Public Wellbeing

Taking proactive steps to reduce street drinking and enhance the safety of our local green spaces like Hill Top Park for families to enjoy.
